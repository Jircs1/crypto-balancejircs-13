About

This site is a small single page application on VueJS. Cryptocurrency rates are obtained from the CoinCap.io API. Balances are stored in your browser's localStorage. Your banace therefore stays private and is not sent or stored on any server. Since no server application is required, this site is hosted on GirHub pages.

Enter the amount you have of each cryptocurrency in each row and click save to save it to your browser storage.
